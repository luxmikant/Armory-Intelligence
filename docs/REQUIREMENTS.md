## ðŸŽ¯ Problem Statement

### Current Challenges
1. **Information Fragmentation** - Firearms knowledge scattered across forums, manuals, outdated websites
2. **Safety Gaps** - 40% of gun owners haven't taken formal safety training
3. **Legal Complexity** - Regulations vary by state/city and change frequently
4. **Accessibility** - Technical manuals intimidating for beginners
5. **Misinformation** - Online forums often contain incorrect or dangerous advice

### Impact
- Thousands of preventable accidents annually
- Legal violations due to lack of awareness
- Knowledge barrier prevents responsible ownership
